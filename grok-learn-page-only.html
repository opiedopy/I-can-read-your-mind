Secrets of the Mind Reader revealed

⚗ Classified Knowledge

This is the inner sanctum. Here you will learn the mathematical magic that makes this trick work — and how to perform it flawlessly in front of an audience.

1

Set The Stage

Ask someone to choose a large number — ideally their phone number with area code (10 digits). Tell them: "Use lots of different digits, don't repeat the same one all the way through." Have them write it down where you cannot see it, or hide the mobile screen if online.

2

The Scramble

Ask them to rearrange those exact digits in any order they like and write that number below the first. Then subtract the smaller from the larger. All without you seeing anything.

3

Choose A Secret Digit

Ask them to look at the result of the subtraction and mentally select any one non-zero digit from it. They keep this completely secret.

4

They Read You The Rest

Now they read out all the other digits in the result — the ones they did NOT choose — one at a time, slowly, in any order. You listen carefully and do the math in your head.

🔮 The Secret: Modulo 9 Magic

When you subtract a number from its scrambled version, the result is always divisible by 9. This is the key mathematical truth. The sum of all its digits will be a multiple of 9.

So as the subject reads out their digits, you add them up in your head using modulo-9 arithmetic. Whatever you're missing to reach 9 (or 0) — that's their secret digit!

# Example walkthrough Original number: 6245123508 Scrambled: 2408513652 Subtraction: 3836609856 ← subject sees this Subject secretly picks: 9 Subject reads aloud: 3, 8, 3, 6, 6, 0, 8, 5, 6 # You think in your head: 3 → 3 3+8 → 11 → 2 (11 mod 9 = 2) 2+3 → 5 5+6 → 11 → 2 2+6 → 8 8+0 → 8 (skip 0) 8+8 → 16 → 7 7+5 → 12 → 3 3+6 → 9 → 0 (9 mod 9 = 0) Running total mod 9: 0 Therefore secret digit = 9 ✓ (0 + 9 = 9, a multiple of 9)

The rule: 9 minus your running total = their secret digit. If your total is already 0, their digit is 9 (since 9 mod 9 = 0).

5

The Mental Math Shortcut

Keep a running total. Whenever it hits 9 or above, subtract 9 (or take mod 9). Zeros are irrelevant — skip them entirely. When they're done reading, your answer is: 9 - total. If total is 0, say 9.

6

The Dramatic Reveal

Close your eyes. Look pensive. Then announce their number with confidence. Practice the mental arithmetic until it's fast. The slower and more theatrical you are, the more magical it appears!

⚠

One Edge Case

The trick requires the subject to choose a non-zero digit. If every digit in the result except one is zero, the trick is trivially obvious. Also, if the number uses all identical digits (e.g. 111111), the scrambled version equals the original and the subtraction gives zero — this is why you instruct them to use varied digits.

★

Why Does This Work?

Any number and its anagram (rearrangement) have the same digit sum, so they have the same value mod 9. Subtracting them gives a result ≡ 0 (mod 9). This beautiful property of our base-10 number system is what makes the whole trick possible — it's pure mathematics wearing a magician's cape.
